Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 16, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how atantawy.com ("Website", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you visit our website. We are committed to transparency and protecting your privacy. This policy describes what minimal data is collected, how it may be used, and your rights regarding your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Direct Collection

We do not directly collect, store, or process any personal information. This website:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not include contact forms
  • Does not require user registration or accounts
  • Does not use local storage or session storage
  • Does not collect email addresses (except when you voluntarily email us)

2.2 GitHub Pages Server Logs

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may automatically collect and store certain technical information in server logs, including:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website
  • Pages visited and access times
  • HTTP request information

This information is collected and controlled by GitHub, Inc. and is subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement. We do not have access to these logs and cannot control how GitHub processes this data.

2.3 Third-Party Resources

This website loads images and resources from third-party domains for performance optimization:

  • Unsplash (images.unsplash.com) - May collect access logs and analytics
  • LinkedIn (media.licdn.com) - May collect access information
  • Wikimedia (upload.wikimedia.org) - May log access requests

When your browser loads these resources, these third parties may receive your IP address, browser information, and the fact that you visited this website. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies:

  • Unsplash Privacy Policy
  • LinkedIn Privacy Policy
  • Wikimedia Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not directly collect or store personal information, we do not use your data for any purpose. The minimal technical data that may be collected by GitHub Pages or third-party services is used solely for:

  • Delivering website content to your browser
  • Maintaining website security and preventing abuse
  • Technical diagnostics and performance optimization

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any personal information because we do not collect it. The only data sharing that occurs is inherent to how websites function:

  • GitHub Pages: Technical logs as described in Section 2.2
  • Third-party resources: Access information when loading external content
  • Legal obligations: If required by law, GitHub may be compelled to disclose server logs to authorities

5. Email Communication

If you choose to contact us via email (website@atantawy.com), we will receive:

  • Your email address
  • Any information you choose to include in your message
  • Email metadata (timestamps, subject line, etc.)

We will use this information solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not add email addresses to mailing lists without explicit consent, and we do not share your email with third parties except as necessary to respond to your request.

6. Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store data directly:

  • Website visit data: Controlled by GitHub's retention policies
  • Email correspondence: Retained only as long as necessary to respond to inquiries or as required for legitimate business purposes

7. Your Rights (GDPR & Data Protection)

Even though we collect minimal data, if you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or other jurisdictions with data protection laws, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request information about any data we may have about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (email correspondence)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation on how data is used
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of processing

For data collected by GitHub Pages, you must contact GitHub directly as they are the data controller for that information.

To exercise your rights regarding any email correspondence, contact us at: website@atantawy.com

8. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Security

We implement reasonable security practices to protect this website:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers where supported
  • No storage of sensitive data on our servers
  • Regular security reviews of website code

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect information, we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted to or from this website.

10. International Data Transfers

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may store and process data in the United States and other countries. By using this website, you acknowledge that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

11. Do Not Track (DNT)

Since we do not track visitors or use analytics, Do Not Track (DNT) browser settings are inherently respected. We do not respond to DNT signals because there is no tracking to disable.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised.

Material changes will be prominently posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

14. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of Austria and the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), where applicable. Any disputes relating to this policy shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Austria.

15. Summary

In plain language: This website is designed to respect your privacy. We don't use cookies, analytics, or any tracking. The only data collected is minimal server logs kept by GitHub (our hosting provider) and standard web browser requests to third-party image hosts. If you email us, we'll only use your email to respond to you.

This privacy policy exists to be transparent and comprehensive about the minimal data that may be collected as a natural part of how websites work, not because we're actively tracking you.
